About

Bottom line, Up-front; I help companies that genuinely want to understand their customers and how to improve their products. I'm not your guy if you want quick fixes or easy answers. If what I'm offering is interesting to you, keep reading.

Businesses rarely have clean data, clear hypotheses, or unlimited time and that's exactly where I work best.

I'm a Data Scientist with deep experience in causal inference, Bayesian modelling, and experimental design. That means I help companies understand not just what is happening, but why and what to do about it.

I work well with messy, low-volume data; reason carefully about uncertainty; and make the kind of risky, high-stakes decisions that other methods can't support.

I've operated across the full project lifecycle: from framing ambiguous problems with senior stakeholders through to delivering end-to-end solutions and mentoring junior data scientists along the way.
